FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

We Always Provide The Best Service

How long does a cleaning service usually take?
The duration depends on the size of the space and the type of service. General cleaning usually takes less time, while deep cleaning or post-renovation cleaning requires more time.
Do I need to be home for every cleaning service?
No, you don’t need to be home. You can provide access instructions in advance, and our trained team will clean your space professionally and securely.
What is the difference between general cleaning and deep cleaning?
General cleaning focuses on regular maintenance to keep your space clean, tidy, and hygienic. It includes tasks like sweeping and mopping floors, dusting surfaces, wiping countertops, and basic bathroom and kitchen cleaning. It’s ideal for weekly or routine cleaning. Deep cleaning is a more thorough and detailed service that targets hidden dirt, grease, and buildup that regular cleaning doesn’t cover. It includes cleaning behind and under furniture, scrubbing tiles and grout, deep bathroom sanitization, removing grease from kitchen areas, and cleaning hard-to-reach spots. It’s best for first-time cleanings, move-in/move-out, post-renovation, or spaces that haven’t been cleaned for a long time.
