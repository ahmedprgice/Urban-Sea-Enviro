Our Story

Urban Sea Enviro began in 2022 with a clear mission: to provide eco-friendly cleaning services that truly make a difference.

Our team, formed by professionals from aviation, construction, and sports science, identified the growing demand for sustainable, high-quality cleaning solutions.

Today, Urban Sea Enviro offers housekeeping, upholstery care, and eco-focused services that create cleaner, healthier spaces while protecting our planet.
